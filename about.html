Our Story

Welcome to Spice Garden

Founded in 2010 by Chef Jashan, Spice Garden has been serving authentic Indian cuisine in the heart of Chandigarh. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the rich flavors and traditions of Indian cooking to our community.

At Spice Garden, we believe in using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our chefs combine traditional recipes with modern techniques to create dishes that are both authentic and innovative.

Whether you're a longtime fan of Indian cuisine or trying it for the first time, we invite you to experience the warmth and hospitality that have made Spice Garden a beloved destination for food lovers in Chandigarh.
